About Our Hotel

"At ‘Hotel Ashik International’, we know what it means to come from a long and tiring trip and to long for a genuine welcome, a good drink and a comfortable bed to stretch out on. If you enjoy being made a fuss of, you’ve come to the right place! We have a total of 121 room and suites to make your stay enjoyable. Families and business people love us, too. We’ve been hosts to many college graduations, family re-unions, birthday celebrations, wedding celebrations, meetings and training programs in 3 of our gorgeous ‘hall rooms’ and 2 full featured meeting rooms. At lobby we have 1 coffee shop, 1 specious restaurant where we serve Thai, Chinese and Bagnoli cuisine. We have a separate B-B-Q station, a Supershop, swimming pool and gym. Our parking facility can accommodate 100+ cars with valet service. Right around the corner from City center, minutes away from Railway station, the bus terminal and Airport. You won't find a better value than this true 3-star Hotel. Come on in. We’re here to make you feel good!"

WHY CHOOSE US?

10 reason to choose our Hotel

1. The convenient location in the city center.
Planning the day, you will not depend on the choice of vehicles. The low traffic congestion within Sadovaya Ring allows you traveling by car. The Bus Terminal of Jessore is 20 meters away from the hotel, it’s can help you be in any district of Bangladesh.

2. The attractions within walking distance.
Our hotel is situated in the center of old Town, and surrounded by worldwide fame monuments, museums, temples, historical buildings.

3. Our price will surprise you.
The hotel “Ashik International” provides rooms of different categories. The wide range of prices can satisfy any guest. Room rates vary from 2000 taka per a day. The hotel has flexible system of discounts.

4. Comfortable accommodation.
All our rooms combine elements of style, elegant and modern facilities and provide you everything for a comfortable stay. All rooms have individual air conditioning, bathroom with a separate bath or shower (depending on the category of room), free Wi-Fi, satellite TV.

5. The high quality service.
The hotel “Ashik International” provides a high quality service for all guests. We have got attentive and well qualified staff, operative check-in / check-out, taxi service, daily housekeeping and change of linen, towels and other services for guests.

6. Special offers for corporate clients.
The hotel “Ashik International” has a system of discounts for corporate clients, tourist groups and regular customers, who prefer early booking.

7. Air and train ticket office.
In our hotel you can book and buy air or train tickets to any destination.

8. English speaking staff.
The hotel provides English speaking staff for foreign guests.

9. Restaurants and cafés.
In the close proximity from our hotel you can enjoy breakfast, business lunch or romantic dinner. There are many restaurants of Chinese, European, Japanese, Mediterranean, Caucasus, Russian cuisine, cozy cafes and pastry shops.

10. Shopping.
The main department stores of the capital for children and adults are situated in close proximity of our hotel. There are the largest bookstore, antique shops, different exhibitions and fairs are held in Gostiny Dvor.

So it’s only first 10 reasons to stay in our hotel…
